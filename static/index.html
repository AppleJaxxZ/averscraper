Getting Started

If you are here, it's because you have to get drug screened from AverHealth and have been given a pin number that you use to check to see if you are scheduled. How this app works is it takes your pin number and date of birth and checks the AverHealth website to see if you have a urine test. The difference between this app and the website is that this app automatically runs once a day for you without having to continuously enter in your login information. As a bonus, this app also runs in the Background of your device and sends you an notification if you are scheduled for a urine screen. As a double bonus, Im also setting up text message alerts as well. This app is intended to be a tool, a layer of protection, and is in no way a fail-safe. This app is in it's infancy and may be prone to errors, bugs, and shutdowns while the first year of testing is happening.

App Features

  1. Runs the app every night at 9:30pm so you know the day before your are scheduled.
  2. Privacy and security at every angle.
  3. Text Message Alerts (COMMING SOON)
  4. Background Notifications (as long as your connected to the internet)
  5. FREEDOM! No more forgetting to call the AverHealth line everyday.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Proceed to login screen.
  2. Enter in your pin number and date of birth
  3. Be patient. This will take a minute for your first call. After your first call you can check the app daily anytime after 9:30pm to check your scheduled status without having to run the call each time.
