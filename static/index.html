Getting Started

If you are here, it's because you have to get urines from AverHealth. What this app does, is takes the hassle out of calling everyday, and lets the client enter in their PIN and Date Of Birth, into the system which is then stored locally on your device and reused to check if you are scheduled for a test today. The test runs every night at 9:30pm so you can know the night before the day of your test if you are being scheduled.
